Devas in tech is a community for women off all ages and colors that want to get into tech. If you just started learning HTML or got your first tech job or are a professional but just started learning another programming language, Devas is for you! Our goal is to provide a safe space where we can ask questions, share struggles, receive support and motivation or just vent, we are here for you and you can be here for other people like you too!
